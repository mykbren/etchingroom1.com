What Are You Waiting for Today?

April 29, 2026

A public art installation, “What Are You Waiting For Today?”, is currently on display at MuseumsQuartier Wien in Vienna. The work explores themes of waiting and human interaction with urban space, inviting visitors to reflect on their daily rhythms and decisions.
