EtchingRoom1 is the printing studio founded in 2016 by two graphic artists Kristina Yarosh and Anna Khodkova in Kyiv (Ukraine). Artists are working in several graphic technics such as linocut, etching, engraving on cardboard, monotype, silk screen printing and creating art books. As a studio, launched educational program and are actively organizing and participating in exhibitions.
