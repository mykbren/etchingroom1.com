Tonight I'm Staying at Home

In the series presented, the fine graphics of classic black and white etching are combined with deliberately “naive” color drawings. The childlike style contrasts with the disturbing plots that unfold in the commuter town of the post-Soviet city.

Tonight I'm Staying at Home is the title of an exhibition at Dymchuk Gallery (Kyiv, Ukraine) that took place from January 31 to March 1, 2020.
